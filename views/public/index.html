About Technorax

Welcome to the Flagship Event of IEEE ADGITM

Acknowledging the theme of IEEE DAY “Leveraging Technology for a Better Tomorrow” and with the motive of taking the world towards a better future, IEEE ADGITM organizes “TECHNORAX” every year on the occasion of IEEE DAY with great enthusiasm and dynamism.

TECHNORAX is the annual flagship event of IEEE ADGITM (formerly known as IEEE NIEC). Students and professionals from all over Delhi- NCR and various other student branches like IEEE MAIT, IEEE DTU, IEEE MSIT, IEEE NSUT etc take active participation. It encourages the students to pursue engineering more as passion rather than profession.
